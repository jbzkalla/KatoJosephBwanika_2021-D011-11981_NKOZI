Kallachick Poultry Breeders Ltd

Kallachick Poultry Breeders Limited is a Ugandan, family owned and run business. Established in 1992, Located in Soweto, about 5 miles North of Masaka. Kallachick Poultry Breeders Limited has five mutually dependent business divisions namely ;- Feed Mill, Parent farm, Hatchery, Broiler Farm, Processing Plant.

The day old chicks are sold to commercial farmers who have done bookings and some of the broilers are reared on Kallachick's owned broiler farms. The Feed Mill produces quality feed for the parent farms, broiler farms and commercial poultry farmers packed in different types and sizes.
